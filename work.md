### Research
I previously did research in computational geometry. As an undergraduate, I found approximation algorithms for variants of the correlation clustering problem. Then in graduate school, I wrote papers on how to find centerpoints (which are high-dimensional generalizations of medians from statistics) and how to measure the "squiggliness" of a curve.
