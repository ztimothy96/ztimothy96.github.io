### Pianos

I started playing piano when I was seven, and it's always been a pretty big part of my life. I used to practice a lot more, and even muddled around in the [Bienen](https://music.northwestern.edu) conservatory for a while before realizing it wasn't for me. You can find some of my recitals and fun recordings [here](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvoxHofhQK_onCBc4nbIpvtJ_V_tYwW7p).

### Composition

After dropping out of music school, I lost access to practice rooms, so I ended up spending a lot of time composing and analyzing Bach instead. It was really fun, and I probably spend more time composing than practicing now (although, since I started working, I don't spend very much time on either).

Most of the compositions I've written are memes, because I don't want to take myself too seriously. For instance, see my [fugue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VD8y_HzRYRQ) on the "Twelve Days of Christmas." Occasionally I write something more ambitious, like a [violin sonata](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7I0ek_gd1Ss).

For a full list of things I've written, see [this](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvoxHofhQK_rE05sAZEpf3KWlpDTRnyNW) playlist.
